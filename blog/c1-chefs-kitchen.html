Dev Bootcamp

Why I Chose to Learn Programming Here

June 14, 2015

The video on the Dev Boocamp experience validated why I chose Dev Bootcamp. I know I will have support if I have any questions or concerns because we are all in this together. Dev Bootcamp seeks to foster a community of developers as opposed to being isolated and at times, frustrated.

Dev Bootcamp is unique in that there is rigorous prep before walking into their doors. The prep is a confidence booster because there is a feeling of “Ok, I at least have background of what is going on.”

Dev Bootcamp wants us to “expose our ignorance,” This means there is always room for questions. There is no need to be afraid of asking a “stupid question.”
